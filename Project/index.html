Bay Area e-Biking Club

This name represents a club that is focused on electric biking in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Optional domain availability: bay-ebike.org

History

We aim to hold an organised ride once a month or so, usually between October and March depending on the weather and work commitments. The local rides will usually be between 15 and 30 miles in length and take between 2 and 5 hours stopping for refreshments at least once along the way. We aim to use smaller, quiet roads for as much of the ride as possible.

The site provides a hub for regional e-biking by providing trail and e-bike recommendation information along with membership information.

The priority of the club is enjoyment and participation which means we will always ride to the pace of the slowest rider. If numbers are high we may split the groups to reduce road congestion and manage pace.
